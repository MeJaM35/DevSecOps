About Us

Welcome to Wealth Wizard, your trusted partner in smart finance tracking. Our mission is to empower individuals to take control of their financial future with AI-powered tools and insights.

With features like expense categorization, forecasting, and a financial assistant, we aim to simplify personal finance management for everyone.

Wealth Wizard is a personal finance management tool developed by Digambar. It helps users manage their income and expenses with ease.

The project includes features like income tracking, expense summaries, financial forecasting, and personalized reports for each family member.

Users can add fixed monthly incomes like salary or business and track daily expenses such as food, travel, and medical costs.

Wealth Wizard also uses machine learning to provide smart insights and includes an AI chatbot for quick financial assistance.

Our goal is to make money management simple, smart, and accessible for everyone.
